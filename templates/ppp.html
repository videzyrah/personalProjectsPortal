Food Preparation (Cooking / Meal Planning)

One of the main problems in my life is figuring out how to feed myself... I hope to help others that struggle similarly by creating recipes and/or meal plans dedicated to single people. I got a mini InstantPot for christmas and I am really enjoying figuring out new recipes that I consider better than what came in the provided recipe book... Basically I want to discuss all the knowledge I wish I had 25 years ago.
